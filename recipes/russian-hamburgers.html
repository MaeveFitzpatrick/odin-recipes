Russian Hamburgers

Description:

This recipe goes back to my grandmother's friend. The recipe has since been modified by my grandma and my mom.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef or Turkey
  • A Slice of Bread
  • Chopped Onion
  • Worchestershire Sauce
  • A Splash of Milk
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • Salad Macaroni

Steps:

  1. Chop onion.
  2. Soak sliced of bread with milk, and break apart.
  3. Combine meat, onion, bread, Worchestershire and salt and pepper, and form into patties.
  4. Boil water for pasta.
  5. Heat oil in skillet, and brown patties.
  6. After some time, add can of cream of mushroom soup and half a can of milk to pan.
  7. Cook and drain pasta, then add to cream of mushroom mixture.
  8. Serve.
