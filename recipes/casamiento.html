Casamiento

Description:

This is a classic Salvadorian recipe of rice and beans.

Ingredients:

  • Red Beans
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Rice
  • Tomato
  • Chicken Bouillon

    • Steps:

    1. Boil beans with a slice of onion, 1 garlic clove and salt for several hours
    2. Add oil to a sauce pan, and saute chopped onion, halved garlic, and chopped tomato.
    3. Brown rice in sauce pan, then add water and chicken bouillon.
    4. Once boiling, lower heat and cook for 20 minutes.
