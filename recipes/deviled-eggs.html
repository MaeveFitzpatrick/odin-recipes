Deviled Eggs

Description:

This is a common hor d'oeurve served at Mahjong.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Mayonaisse
  • Vinegar
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Paprika

Steps:

  1. Hard boil eggs.
  2. Blend egg yolk, mayo, vinegar, mustard and salt.
  3. Place mixture in bag, and pipe into egg white halves.
  4. Top with paprika.
